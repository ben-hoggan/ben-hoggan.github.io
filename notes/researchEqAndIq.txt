What is Emotional Intelligence?
The ability to identify, empathize and manage emotions. It overlaps with skills like self awareness, social skills, and relationship management.

How is it different to IQ?
IQ is a measure of an individual's "intelligence" relative to the population but it measures intelligence as problem solving and reasoning ability.
IQ could be said to be intelligence of managing things while EQ would be intelligence of managing people.

Why is it important?
In working as a developer [and most other workplaces] you will operate as a team. 
The ability to effectively communicate and form strong working relationships will improve the work culture.
In personal life having the skills to better empathize with friends and family will improve personal relationships as well.
